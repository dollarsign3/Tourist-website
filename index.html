Culture and Festivals

Sri Lanka is among the few countries in the world with such a diverse cultural landscape. As a result of the country's distinct culture, global visitors flock to Sri Lanka in greater numbers. The most notable element of Sri Lankan culture is its vibrant celebration. Religion has a significant influence on Sri Lankan culture and traditions. The primary nationalities of Sri Lanka are Sinhalese, Tamil, Catholic, and Muslim, and their faiths are Buddhism, Hinduism, Christianity, and Islam, respectively.
