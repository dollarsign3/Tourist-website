Beaches In Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, which is quickly earning international recognition as one of the world's most beautiful beach destinations, provides a diverse range of scenery, from jungles to infinite miles of pristine white-sand beaches.

Nilaveli Beach

Best hotel nearby:

Nilaveli Beach is a wide, sandy beach with coconut palms and fishing boats sprinkled about. It's a 30-minute drive from the city of Trincomalee in the northeastern portion of Sri Lanka. Despite its size, Nilaveli's beach is popular with both residents and visitors. There are lifeguards on duty, as well as several small eateries along the beach, most of which specialize in seafood.

Arugam Bay Beach

Best hotel nearby:

Arugam Bay is stunning, with sparkling white sand and a deep blue sea that gradually turns green as you move closer to the beach. The beach is recognized as a worldwide surf destination because of its attractiveness, which includes some of Sri Lanka's top surf breakers. The Spice Trail is a nice, tiny luxury resort with private villa rooms right on Arugam Bay. Each unit has its own tiny outside garden space, ideal for unwinding in a lounge chair. Ancient temples and Kumana National Park, famed for the numerous types of birds that dwell in or migrate through the area, are both neighboring attractions.

Unawatuna Beach

Best hotel nearby:

Unawatuna is one of Sri Lanka's most popular tourist sites and the country's most "renowned" beach. It's a beautiful banana-shaped beach with golden sand and blue sea, all encircled by lush green palm trees! Unawatuna and its beach are located near Galle, a colonial town. The beach is popular with visitors, and there are loungers for rent on part of the beach. There are some rocks and stairs at the beach's west end that lead up to a tiny hill with a pagoda and a large Buddha statue. It's a pleasant short stroll with a lovely view of the shore.
